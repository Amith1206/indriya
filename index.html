ABOUT INDRIYA

INDRIYA is a State level Project Development competition for students from classes 7th to 12th. We aim to improve the students’ skill in ideation, while mentoring and guiding them along every step of the way, in order to help their innovative ideas take shape. Indriya will consist of two rounds: the first where the students are to form teams and submit abstracts on given problem statements. The second round is where the shortlisted students are to develop their projects with technical training and professional mentorship from volunteers throughout the rest of the program.

So here is how its done...

Ideation Phase :

  • ● Develop an idea based on any one of the problem statements given below.

  • ● Draft an abstract of your idea with reference to the given template:
    <template link>
    (Please make sure that the abstracts are sent in the same given template. This is to ensure fair competition and evaluation.)

  • ● Send in your entries to ieee@mec.ac.in on or before 15th May 2020

  • ● Selected projects will be qualified for the next phase.

Project Development Phase :

Selected projects will be assigned mentors who will help you in completing the project by providing resources and guidance

A detailed template will be provided which should be updated on a weekly basis, enabling the mentors to track your progress.

Participants are expected to complete the project by the end of June since the tentative dates for the project presentation could change with the social situation.

Problem statements :

  • Track 01 : Environmental Issues

    Waste Disposal and Management
    Disaster Mitigation
    Natural Resource Utilisation

  • Track 02 : Social Issues

    Road safety and Accident Prevention
    Smart Agricultural Practices/Agritech
    Clean Water Provision

  • Track 03 : Human Health

    Prevention and control of Pandemics
    Adulteration prevention/Healthy Alternatives
